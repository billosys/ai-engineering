# Competency Questions for Rust Style Guide

1. What is the Rust Style Guide?
2. What are Rust's formatting conventions (indentation, line width)?
3. What are Rust's naming conventions?
4. What is a style edition?
5. What are the Cargo.toml formatting conventions?
6. What are the guiding principles of the Rust style?

## Procedural (How do I do X?)
7. How do I format function definitions in Rust?
8. How do I format match expressions?
9. How do I format method chains?
10. How do I format closures?
11. How do I format struct/enum definitions?
12. How do I format if/else expressions?
13. How do I format Cargo.toml files?

## Diagnostic (What distinguishes X from Y?)
14. What is the difference between block indent and visual indent?
15. What is the difference between formatting items vs expressions?
16. When should I use a trailing comma?
