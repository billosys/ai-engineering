Advocate is an assertive all-caps display face, especially good for <a href="business-cards.html" class="xref">business cards</a>, <a href="letterhead.html" class="xref">letterhead</a>, logos, and titles. I use it throughout this book for <span class="no-hyphens">headlines.</span>

1.  A total of 18 styles: three widths (= medium, semibold, bold) and three weights (= condensed, narrow, regular), in both sans and slab-serif <span class="no-hyphens">versions.</span>

2.  Advocate has a short, plain-English [font license](https://mbtype.com/license) that includes permission to use it in PDFs, websites, apps, and electronic <span class="no-hyphens">books.</span>

3.  Advocate has no demo version, but I do offer a 30-day return option: if the fonts aren’t your style, you can cancel your license for a <span class="no-hyphens">refund.</span>
