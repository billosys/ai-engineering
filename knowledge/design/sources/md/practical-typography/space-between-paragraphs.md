space between paragraphsUse 4–10 points

Space between paragraphs is an alternative to a <a href="first-line-indents.html" class="xref">first-line indent</a> for signaling the start of a new <span class="no-hyphens">paragraph.</span>

The worst way to put space between paragraphs is to insert an extra carriage return. (See <a href="carriage-returns.html" class="xref">carriage returns</a> for <span class="no-hyphens">why.)</span>

As with first-line indents, you want the space to be large enough to be easily noticed, but not so large that the paragraphs seem disconnected. A space equal to 50–100% of the <a href="body-text.html" class="xref">body text</a> size will usually suffice. The larger the <a href="point-size.html" class="xref">point size</a>, the more space you’ll need between paragraphs to make a visible <span class="no-hyphens">difference.</span>

<div class="howto">

<div class="howto-name">

How to set space between paragraphs

</div>

WordRight-click in the text and select Paragraph → Indents and Spacing. Under Spacing, in the box next to After, enter the <span class="no-hyphens">measurement.</span>

PagesView → Show Toolbar (or option + ⌘ + t) → Format button → Style pane → under Spacing, in the box labeled After Paragraph, enter the <span class="no-hyphens">measurement.</span>

CSSUse the `margin-top` and `margin-bottom` <span class="no-hyphens">properties</span>

</div>

<div class="btw">

<div class="btw-title">

by the way

</div>

- Is space before a paragraph equivalent to space after? Sometimes. In word processors, the space between two paragraphs is the *larger* of the space after the first paragraph and the space before the second paragraph. Thus, if every paragraph has 12 points of space after, you’ll get 12 points of space between each pair. But if each paragraph has 6 points of space before and 6 points after, the space between will only be 6 points. To avoid surprises, I prefer to rely on space after, and use space before in special circumstances. For instance, a <a href="block-quotations.html" class="xref">block quotation</a> may need space before and after to look vertically <span class="no-hyphens">aligned.</span>
