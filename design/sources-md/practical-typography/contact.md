Contact

You can reach me by email, but please <span class="no-hyphens">note—</span>

1.  I don’t hand out design templates, including the source files for the <a href="sample-documents.html" class="xref">sample documents</a>. You’re welcome, however, to crib as many ideas as you <span class="no-hyphens">like.</span>

2.  I can’t dispense individualized typographic advice (e.g., “What font should I use for … ?”) The point of this book, after all, is to help you make those decisions <span class="no-hyphens">yourself.</span>

3.  “I don’t want to [pay for your book.](how-to-pay-for-this-book.md) I just want to express my outrage!” Please—no need. You’ve already [voted with your <span class="no-hyphens">wallet.</span>](vote-with-your-wallet.md)

Fair enough? OK then—I’m at <a href="mailto:mb@mbtype.com" class="xref">mb@mbtype.com</a>.
