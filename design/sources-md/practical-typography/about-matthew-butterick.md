About Matthew Butterick

I’m a writer, typographer, programmer, and <span class="no-hyphens">lawyer.</span>

I designed the fonts used in this book—see <a href="mb-fonts.html" class="xref">MB fonts</a>.

In addition to *Practical Typography*, I also wrote the books [*Typography for Lawyers*](https://typographyforlawyers.com) and [*Beautiful Racket*.](https://beautifulracket.com)

To make these books possible, I created [Pollen,](https://pollenpub.com) an open-source publishing system. Pollen was built with the world’s most interesting programming language, [Racket.](https://racket-lang.org)

I have a visual-studies degree from Harvard University, and a law degree from UCLA. I live in Los Angeles with my wife <span class="no-hyphens">Jessica.</span>
