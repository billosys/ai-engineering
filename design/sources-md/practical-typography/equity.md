</div>

Equity is one of the fonts available for <a href="body-text.html" class="xref">body text</a> in this book. (Click on the font-selection toolbar at the bottom of the page to try it.) Equity was originally designed for legal writers, but any professional writer can benefit from its many <span class="no-hyphens">conveniences:</span>

Equity fits as much text on the page as <a href="times-new-roman-alternatives.html" class="xref">Times New Roman</a>, and stays legible down to small <a href="point-size.html" class="xref">point sizes</a>.

Equity has been designed to perform well on both high-end output devices and office printers. Each style in the family comes in two weight grades so you can pick the grade that looks just right on your printer—a feature found in very few <span class="no-hyphens">fonts.</span>

Equity comes with a separate set of caps fonts, which contain real <a href="small-caps.html" class="xref">small caps</a> and already include my recommended <a href="letterspacing.html" class="xref">letterspacing</a>.

Build your own font bundle: your Equity order will include a discount code to get additional families for \$79 instead of \$119 (1–2 person license only). You can also upgrade anytime to the full MB Type Library for the difference in <span class="no-hyphens">price.</span>

Equity has a short, plain-English [font license](https://mbtype.com/license) that includes permission to use it in PDFs, websites, apps, and electronic books. It is a one-time payment, not a subscription.

Each license covers multiple people, but only within a single organization or family. A license cannot be split among friends or random strangers. [Details here.](https://mbtype.com/license)

Equity has no demo version, but I offer a 30-day return option: if the fonts aren’t your style, you can cancel your license for a <span class="no-hyphens">refund.</span>
