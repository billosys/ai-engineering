Like Cambria, Calibri works well on screen. But in print, its rounded corners make body text look soft. If you need a clean sans serif font, you have better options. My font , for instance. <a href="fontrec/guardian-sans.html" class="xref">Guardian Sans</a> is the companion to Guardian Egyptian (see <a href="cambria-alternatives.html" class="xref">Cambria alternatives</a>). They’re named after the newspaper that originally had them designed. <a href="fontrec/seravek.html" class="xref">Seravek</a> and <a href="fontrec/fort.html" class="xref">Fort</a> are both sharper than Calibri—Seravek is similarly narrow, while Fort is wider and more <span class="no-hyphens">open.</span>
